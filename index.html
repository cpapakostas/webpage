Research Associate (NKUA) • Postdoctoral Researcher (UniWA)

About

I work at the intersection of Religious Education, Artificial Intelligence, and Augmented/Mixed Reality, with a focus on learning design, ethics of AI in education, and technology‑enhanced cultural heritage.

Research

AI in Religious Education

Pedagogical, ethical, and theological perspectives; frameworks aligned with policy and practice.

AR/MR for Learning

Spatial ability training and experiential learning for Church History and cultural heritage.
